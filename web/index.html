Privacy

Local-first history, with Apple system features when you choose them.

Sunclub stores app history on device, can use your private iCloud database for sync, and lets you export a local backup when you want a copy.

  • No app-owned accounts.
  • No ads or third-party analytics SDKs.
  • Optional notifications, location features, and HealthKit.
  • Optional private iCloud sync and local backup export.
  • Shortcut writes, URL writes, and callback details can be disabled in Settings.
